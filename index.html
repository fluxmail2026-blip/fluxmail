How it works
Simple by design.
Powerful in practice.
Step 01
Address is ready instantly

Open FluxMail and your disposable address is already generated. No forms, no password. Just land and go.

Step 02
Use it wherever you need

Copy and paste it into any signup form. Receive confirmation links and verification codes without giving away your real email.

Step 03
It disappears on its own

After 10 minutes the address self-destructs with every message in it. No traces, no spam, nothing stored on our servers.

TLS 1.3 encrypted

All traffic is secured end to end. Your temporary inbox gets the same protection as your permanent one.

Multiple domains

Spam shield

Every address is fully isolated. Sign up for anything without polluting your real inbox.

REST API

Generate addresses and poll inboxes programmatically. Clean JSON responses, no auth required.
