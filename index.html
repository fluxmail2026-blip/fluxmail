FAQ
Common questions.
Straight answers.
What is a temporary email address?

A temporary email address (also called disposable email, temp mail, or throwaway email) is a short-lived inbox you can use instead of your real email. It receives messages normally but auto-deletes after a set time, keeping your real inbox clean.

Is FluxMail completely free?

Yes, 100% free forever. No hidden fees, no premium plans, no credit card required. FluxMail is supported by non-intrusive advertising.

How long does a temporary email last?

Each FluxMail address is active for 10 minutes by default. You can extend it by clicking "+10 min" as many times as you need, up to 60 minutes total.

Can I use FluxMail to sign up for websites?

Yes. FluxMail works perfectly for email verifications, newsletter signups, free trials, and any service that requires an email address. Some large platforms like Google or Facebook block known disposable email domains — this is normal and affects all temp mail services.

Is my temporary email private and anonymous?

Completely. We store zero personal data, no IP addresses, no browsing history. Every inbox auto-deletes with all its messages. There is no way to trace a FluxMail address back to you.

What is the difference between FluxMail and Guerrilla Mail or Mailinator?

FluxMail is faster, cleaner and more private. Unlike Guerrilla Mail or Mailinator, your inbox is not publicly searchable — only you can access it during your session. The interface is also significantly simpler and mobile-friendly.
